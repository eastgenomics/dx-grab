Prefers terse, direct responses with no padding. Gives short instructions and expects them to be considered throughtfully, explained and then acted on.

**Why:** Demonstrated throughout this session — instructions like "update readme", "rename the tool", "commit, push" with no elaboration, and no pushback on concise responses.

**How to apply:** Think carefully and plan ahead to avoid mistakes, provide some explanation and then act.
